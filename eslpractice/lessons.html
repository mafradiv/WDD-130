Lessons

The other resources they find they are great, but they are not designed to this group of people. They may be good and help until a certain point, but they are not helpful at the very beginning. This website is going to help the user on how to begin and then it will give them options to recommend others about where to start. There is a good number of websites they offer great activities but one of the challenges a very low-level student face is they do not understand the vocabulary in the very basic exercise they intend to complete. This is why many institutions that teach English as a second language try to begin with a pre basic class, but it is not profitable for them because the people who need this class do not have the money to afford it. This website will help them. Question I have is how can this website be afforded so it can benefit the students and it can be profitable"English from scratch" will guide you to use material it already exists in the web to understand the lessons better. First you will see photos with the new words you will use in the lesson. then you click on the link that will take you to the lesson. After the lesson click on the link that will take you to a short evaluation. To begin click on the photo of the lesson.
