Overview

Purpose

The objective of this website is to give English students classified as false beginners the help they need to learn the necessary vocabulary, grammar to be considered real beginners. This website will give them photos and grammar exercises and explanations that will help them understand in a very basic way to overcome the poor literacy they may have in their first language but with English. This website will help them think in English. this website will use photos to help students understand the meaning of very basic words. This website will also help them use they very basic expressions they acquired to acquire more advanced ones.

Audience

People in any country and in any language who are iliterate in their own language. People who have difficult acces to technology. People who struggle to satisfy their basic needs but they learned that Enlgish can help them to get better jobs in some areas. English can be the difference between life or dead.

Content

Home page

Why will people come to your site? There is a great deal of people in the world who do not have enough money to afford any kind of education. They do not have the resources and they may have one cellphone and scarce access to internet. They may use the access of any public place where internet access is granted but not a place where you pay money but literally a public place like a park or the avenue or a library. They only have a cellphone. They want to learn English. There are thousands of videos on YouTube where they find explanations, but the explanations are in English, and they do not understand it. There are another number of tutorials in Spanish and castellano. They also teach English, but They are not helping the students since they are giving explanations in castellano to understand English relying on translation as a main way to acquire English. English Is the main language on the web and There are numberless of websites created to learn English. They are helping a lot of people with some background on education but there is still a number of people who do not have access to these resources because they were not trained in learning even in their own language. They will come to this website because the very first steps they need to give. They will find it here and they will also find sources it will help them to understand other learning pages. Word will not be the main way of communicating on this website but photos. What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them? The very first steps to understand the very basic vocabulary to understand English. They will come and see they need to follow certain steps and they can use what they previously learned in this page to understand the following. The first question they will answer here is what do I need to understand? I need to focus on the meaning, the pronunciation, and the use of a word in the target language and how to use that word in a sentence to convey meaning and not the translation. They will discover that translation is mechanical. They can do it but mentally and they will focus on reinforcing the target language. What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site? They will find the first steps previous to understand more complicated activities in their learning journey. What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers? They are going to click in the correct answer to continue to the next activity Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions? The other resources they find they are great, but they are not designed to this group of people. They may be good and help until a certain point, but they are not helpful at the very beginning. This website is going to help the user on how to begin and then it will give them options to recommend others about where to start. There is a good number of websites they offer great activities but one of the challenges a very low-level student face is they do not understand the vocabulary in the very basic exercise they intend to complete. This is why many institutions that teach English as a second language try to begin with a pre basic class, but it is not profitable for them because the people who need this class do not have the money to afford it. This website will help them. Question I have is how can this website be afforded so it can benefit the students and it can be profitable?
